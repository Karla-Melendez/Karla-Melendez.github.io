## 🌿 Aquascape Vision

<a href="https://github.com/Karla-Melendez/Aquascape-Vision" 
target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">View the repository</a>

I am an aquarist. I have spent years designing and maintaining 
planted aquariums, and I know firsthand how much time, effort, and 
money goes into building a successful aquascape — and how often 
things don't turn out the way you imagined because there was no 
good way to visualize the result before committing to it.

Aquascape Vision is the app I have always wished existed.

---

## 🎯 The Problem

Designing a planted aquarium means making dozens of decisions before 
you can see the final result. Plants are purchased small and immature. 
Hardscape materials are expensive and hard to return. Layouts that 
look right on paper may not work in practice. And the information 
you need — plant compatibility, growth habits, placement guidelines, 
design principles — is scattered across forums, videos, retailer 
websites, and social media.

Most aquarium apps focus on maintenance, livestock tracking, and 
water parameters. Almost none address what aquascapers actually 
struggle with most: **designing the aquascape itself**.

---

## 💡 The Vision

Aquascape Vision is a planning and visualization application for 
freshwater planted aquarium enthusiasts. The long-term vision is 
to provide aquascapers with a digital environment where they can:

- Create digital representations of their aquariums
- Arrange plants, driftwood, rocks, and decorative elements
- Explore different aquascaping styles and layouts
- Access a comprehensive aquatic plant knowledge base
- Predict how a layout may evolve as plants mature
- Experiment freely before committing to purchases or physical 
  changes

Over time, the platform may expand to include growth simulation, 
augmented reality visualization, shopping integration, and 
personalized design recommendations.

The goal is to become the most trusted digital planning and 
visualization platform in the planted aquarium hobby — empowering 
aquascapers to design with confidence and bring their ideas to 
life before a single plant is purchased or placed.

---

## 📋 Project Status

This project is in its early planning stages. The repository 
currently contains the foundational documentation guiding the 
app's development:

- **Product Vision** — the problem the app solves and the 
  audience it serves
- **User Personas** — profiles of aquascapers across experience 
  levels, from beginners to advanced hobbyists and professionals
- **Feature Brainstorm** — an evolving list of potential features 
  and capabilities
- **Roadmap** — the planned development milestones and priorities

**Current focus (next 1–2 months):**

- Research existing aquarium and aquascaping apps — documenting 
  their strengths, weaknesses, and feature gaps
- Draft the first version of the plant schema, defining the 
  attributes the app will need to know about each plant
- Create a plant database and populate it with up to 15 of the 
  most commonly used aquatic plants
- Define core entities: tank, hardscape, and layout
- Sketch initial wireframes for the tank planner and plant detail 
  screens

These documents and deliverables will evolve as the project 
progresses.
